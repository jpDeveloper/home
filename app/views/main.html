Jesus Parlange

Web Designer & Developer

Jesus Parlange is a Web Developer from Vancouver, British Columbia. His passion for the arts have lead him to experience creativity in different ways. Starting his adventure in the world of music when he was a kid, J.P. began to write and record his own music with the knowledge acquired within the music academy he attended as a child. Ever since then music as been a huge part of his life. He has always expressed his thoughts and ideas through art. University brought J.P. many awesome opportunities to expand his creative pallet.

Here he was exposed to film, graphic design and photography, thus opening new doors and expanding his skill set for future creations. J.P.'s dedication for music lead him into a successful career as a DJ for 5 years, where he got to travel and share stages with the best DJs around the world. Making the most out of this musical momentum he graduated from sound engineering school and began working with bands and artists across Canada. He has received several awards in the music industry for his work as a music producer including a Juno Award in 2010.

Following his work as a studio engineer and music producer, his experience with computers, creative software and graphic apps took J.P. on another fantastic adventure into the world of Web Development. He brings a unique perspective and skill set to his clients projects, as he integrates all his talents and excitement for graphic design, photography, video and music production into web development projects.

He is known for his ability to easily translate ideas from creative conception into the computer realm, for clearly understanding his clients needs and for his ability to effectively communicate each clients unique message into the world through the utilization of appropriate web based tools. His positive outlook combined with his expansive and flexible skill set, has established him a reputation as reliable, trustworthy and successful throughout his vibrant and diverse career.
